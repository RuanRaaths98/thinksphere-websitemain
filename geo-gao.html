FAQ

What people ask before starting GEO.

Is GEO replacing SEO?

No. GEO builds on SEO. If your site is hard to crawl, thin on proof, or unclear about what you do, AI search systems inherit those same problems.

What is the difference between AEO and GEO?

AEO focuses on direct answers in search results and assistants. GEO focuses on how generative AI systems understand, cite, and recommend your brand inside longer responses.

Can this help a local South African business?

Yes. Local intent, service area clarity, reviews, business profiles, location pages, and category authority all influence whether a brand feels relevant enough to surface.

How soon can we see movement?

Technical and content improvements can ship quickly, but citation trust compounds. We usually start with a 30-day sprint, then refine based on ranking, AI mention, and lead data.
