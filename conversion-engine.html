FAQ

What people ask before starting CRO.

Do we need more traffic before CRO?

Not always. If your existing traffic is qualified, clearer pages and cleaner forms can create meaningful gains before media spend increases.

Do you redesign whole websites?

We can, but the first move is often a focused landing page or funnel path rather than a full rebuild.

How do you choose tests?

We prioritize by likely impact, confidence from data, implementation effort, and business relevance.

Can you improve speed and tracking too?

Yes. Front-end performance and measurement quality are part of the conversion system.
